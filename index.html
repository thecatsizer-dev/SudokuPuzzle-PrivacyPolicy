Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Sprint ("we," "our," or "the App"), developed by CatSizer Lab. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using Sudoku Sprint, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Key Principle: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Google Play Games Profile: When you connect to Google Play Games Services, we access your Player ID (anonymized), display name, and avatar (if available).
  • Friend Codes: You can generate and share a unique friend code (8-digit format) to connect with other players.
  • Game Preferences: Theme selection, language, sound/vibration settings, and difficulty preferences are stored locally on your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Game Statistics Track wins, playtime, achievements Local + Google Play Saved Games
Multiplayer Session Data Match opponents, sync game state Temporary (server-side, not stored)
Leaderboard Scores Global/friend rankings Google Play Games Services
In-Progress Game Saves Resume your game later Local + Cloud Sync (Google Play)

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Games Services: Handles authentication, leaderboards, achievements, and cloud saves. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Displays rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Render.com (Multiplayer Server): Hosts our real-time multiplayer server. Session data (player ID, name) is temporary and not stored permanently.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Provide Core Gameplay: Solo mode, multiplayer matchmaking, game saves.
  2. Social Features: Friend codes, leaderboards, achievements.
  3. Personalization: Save your theme, language, and preferences.
  4. Cloud Synchronization: Sync progress across devices via Google Play Games.
  5. Rewarded Ads: Show optional ads to unlock in-game hints.
  6. Improve the App: Identify bugs, optimize performance (no analytics tracking tools used).
We DO NOT:
  • Sell your personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking tools (no Firebase Analytics, no third-party trackers)
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other sensitive device data
  • Send marketing emails or notifications without consent

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • Google Play Games: Your public profile (name, avatar) is visible to other players in leaderboards and multiplayer matches.
  • Friend Codes: When you share your friend code, other players can send you friend requests.

4.2 Service Providers

  • Google (Play Games + AdMob): Processes authentication, cloud saves, leaderboards, and ad delivery.
  • Render.com: Hosts our multiplayer server (session data only, no permanent storage).

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All network communication uses HTTPS/WSS (WebSocket Secure).
  • Google Play Games: Authentication and cloud saves use Google's secure infrastructure.
  • Local Storage: Data stored on your device is protected by Android's app sandboxing.
  • No Sensitive Data: We do NOT collect payment information, social security numbers, or health data.

Important: While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Use strong device passwords and keep your software updated.

7. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Sprint is designed for general audiences and does NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without parental consent.

  • COPPA Compliance (US): We do not target children under 13.
  • Google Play Games: Users must have a Google account, which requires age verification by Google.
  • Parental Controls: Parents can manage their child's Google account and restrict multiplayer features.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@catsizerlab.com.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

8.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data stored by us.
  • Deletion: Delete your local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling. To delete Google Play data, manage your Google account settings.
  • Opt-Out of Ads: You can skip rewarded ads (you won't receive the in-game rewards).
  • Disconnect Google Play: Sign out from the app settings to disconnect your Google account.

8.2 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA)

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (e.g., update your Google Play profile).
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities.

8.3 CCPA Rights (California, USA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it's used.
  • Request deletion of personal information.
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do NOT sell data).
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.
To Exercise Your Rights:
Contact us at privacy@catsizerlab.com or use the in-app "Data & Privacy" settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Sudoku Sprint is available globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States (where our multiplayer server is hosted via Render.com) and Ireland (where Google servers are located).

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Google's EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification.
  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers.
  • Encryption during data transmission.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify you via an in-app notification or email (if available).
  • Request your consent if required by law.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
